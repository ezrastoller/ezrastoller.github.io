Portfolio

Hi, I'm Ezra Stoller. I'm a 2015 graduate of Harvard College, and am living in Lima for the year studying the Chinese diaspora in Peru as a Trustman Traveling Fellow. Read more about me here. I have studied Chinese brush painting and calligraphy for fifteen years and am also painting a collection of watercolors of Peruvian everyday life with Chinese materials (rice paper, Chinese ink). Take a look at some my art samples here.

Here are some recent writing samples.

  • A Good Sport (2016) – about my experiences as a coxswain at Harvard.
  • Pet Custody (2016) – my Peruvian host family's dog fathered six puppies - now what?
  • To Be or Not To Be (2015) – comparing the aging of my two grandmothers.
  • Two Households Both Alike in Dignity (2015) – reflections on living with Peruvian host family who has a live-in housekeeper.
  • Nonagenarian Joy (2015) – about my grandmother's love for the card game, bridge.
  • Fair Ball (2015) – about the use of technology to umpire baseball.
